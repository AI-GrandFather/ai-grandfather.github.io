User Data Deletion

This page explains how Block Scramble users can request deletion of account-related or integration-related data, including data associated with Facebook Login if such integration is used.

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Block Scramble is designed to minimize data collection. If you believe data associated with your account or app usage should be deleted, you may request deletion using the instructions below.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email us at:

atharmushtaq9@gmail.com

Please use the subject line:

Data Deletion Request – Block Scramble

2. Information to Include

Please include enough information for us to identify your request, such as:

  • Your name or nickname used in the app, if applicable
  • Your email address associated with the request
  • Your Facebook account ID, if you used Facebook Login
  • A short description of the data you want deleted

3. What We Will Delete

Where applicable and legally permissible, we will delete:

  • App account-related information associated with your request
  • Connected login-related data we control
  • Support records tied to the request, where deletion is permitted

4. What May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements
  • Maintain security, fraud prevention, or audit records

5. Processing Time

We aim to review and process valid deletion requests within 7 business days, although complex requests may take longer when legally necessary.

6. Facebook / Meta Users

If you used Facebook Login with Block Scramble, you may also remove the app from your Facebook settings. Removing the app from Facebook disconnects future access, while emailing us allows you to request deletion of data under our control.
