User Data Deletion

This page explains how users of Block Scramble can request deletion of data associated with Facebook Login or other account-linked services, where applicable.

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

1. Overview

Block Scramble aims to minimize data collection. Most gameplay data is stored locally on your device. If you use Facebook Login or other linked services, certain account-related data may be processed to support authentication and related features.

2. How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of data associated with your Block Scramble account or Facebook-linked profile, email us at:

Email: atharmushtaq9@gmail.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request – Block Scramble

3. Information to Include

Please include enough information for us to identify your request, such as:

  • Your name or nickname used in the app, if available
  • Your email address associated with the request
  • Your Facebook account ID or profile email, if you used Facebook Login
  • A short note describing the data you want deleted

4. What We May Delete

  • Facebook Login related identifiers or profile links under our control
  • Support records tied to your request, where deletion is permitted
  • Any server-side account linkage data we maintain for login functionality

5. What May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where legally necessary for:

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Legal compliance
  • Dispute resolution
  • Enforcement of agreements

6. Processing Time

We aim to review and process valid deletion requests within 7 business days, though some requests may take longer if additional verification is required.

7. Disconnecting from Facebook

If you used Facebook Login, you may also remove Block Scramble from your Facebook account settings. Removing the app there disconnects future access, while contacting us allows you to request deletion of data under our control.
