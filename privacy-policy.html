📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Block Scramble ("the App") handles user data. The App is developed and published by AI Grandfather / Mian Muhammad Athar. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Important: Block Scramble is designed as an offline-first game. Most gameplay happens locally on your device. However, the App may use optional third-party services for login, advertising, consent handling, and in-app purchases.

🔐 Facebook Login

Block Scramble may offer Facebook Login so you can sign in more conveniently. If you choose to use Facebook Login, we may receive certain information from Meta, depending on the permissions you approve and the data Meta makes available to the app.

This may include:

  • Your Facebook user identifier
  • Your name
  • Your email address, if shared by Meta and permitted by you
  • Your profile picture, if shared by Meta and permitted by you

We only use Facebook Login data for authentication, account linking, and related in-app features. We do not sell your Facebook profile data.

🛍️ In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

The App may include optional in-app purchases and subscriptions. All transactions are processed securely through the Apple App Store.

We do not receive or store your payment card or billing information. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.

📊 Data We Collect

We aim to collect as little data as possible. The App does not require you to manually create a traditional account with a password.

Depending on which features you use, certain data may be collected automatically by us and our service providers. This may include:

  • Login data associated with Facebook Login or similar authentication methods
  • Device identifiers such as Advertising ID / IDFA or similar identifiers
  • Ad-related information such as ad impressions, clicks, and performance information
  • Approximate location information inferred from IP address or regional settings
  • Usage information such as app launches, session length, and feature interactions
  • Crash or diagnostic information if collected through third-party SDKs or platform tools

We try to avoid collecting directly identifying information unless needed for a feature you choose, such as login.

🎯 Advertising, Personalization & Consent

The App may use Google AdMob and Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to show ads and manage consent where required by law.

Consent screen: On first launch, and later when required, you may see a privacy or consent message asking about personalized advertising choices.

Depending on your choice:

  • If you consent to personalized ads: Ad providers may use certain identifiers and usage data to show more relevant ads.
  • If you do not consent: you may receive non-personalized ads that are based on more limited contextual or regional information.

📱 IDFA & App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the App may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before using the device advertising identifier (IDFA).

  • If you allow tracking, ad services may use IDFA to help deliver and measure personalized ads.
  • If you decline, the App will respect your choice and use limited or non-personalized advertising methods where applicable.

👶 Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We use reasonable steps to protect data handled by the app and the services we rely on. Gameplay progress and many settings may remain stored locally on your device unless a linked account or cloud-related feature is used.
